# comments to rhyme transcriptions (sorted by importance)
	1) should diphtongs (*au/ai/oi/əi/ui) be transcribed in one glyph or two? Current solution: two;
	2) potential conflict in coda *-t/-(t)s: is transcription as ㄉ (used as initial t/d etc.) adequate? 
	3) potential conflict in rhymes *ap/ep, *am/em, *əp/ip, *əm/im: in each rhyme, vowel is transcribed as first in pair -- what about second (*e, *i)?
	4) potential conflict in rhymes *oi/wai, *ot/wat/os/was, *on/wan, *ui/wəi, *un/wən: one vowel or diphtong?
